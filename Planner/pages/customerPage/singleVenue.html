About Briarhurst Manor Estate

Only a few minutes drive west of Colorado Springs, The Briarhurst Manor is located in the tiny valley of Manitou Springs. The stately Victorian Manor House was build in 1876 by the founder of Manitou Springs, Dr. William Bell, of London, England. Briarhurst Manor Estate is a full service restaurant and we serve the finest Colorado Cuisine in an amazing atmosphere with spectacular views of Pike's Peak, very close to Garden of the Gods.
